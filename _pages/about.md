I am an AI and Machine Learning Engineer with a passion for leveraging technology to solve complex challenges in healthcare and material science. Having recently completed my Master's degree in IT (specializing in intelligent systems, computer vision, and data science) from Åbo Akademi University, I am now employed full-time on the Horizon Europe **NAP4DIVE** project.

My expertise lies in the entire machine learning lifecycle, from advanced data analysis and fine-tuning large language models (LLMs) to implementing and deploying predictive models using MLOps best practices. I am proficient in deep learning frameworks like PyTorch and TensorFlow and committed to the principles of AI fairness and explainability.

My background includes hands-on experience developing EEG-based predictive models, designing ML-driven analytics dashboards, and contributing to research in fields ranging from brain-computer interfaces to apnea prediction in premature infants. I am driven to apply my skills to create efficient, high-impact AI solutions.
